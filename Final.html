Exploring the Allegheny Mountains

Overview

The Allegheny Mountains are a major mountain range in the eastern United States, part of the larger Appalachian Mountains system. They span parts of Pennsylvania, Maryland, West Virginia and Virginia.

Scenic View

Interesting Features

Some notable features of the Alleghenies include:

  • The dramatic escarpment known as the Allegheny Front.
  • Remote wilderness areas with unique high-altitude ecosystems.
  • Historical importance as a barrier to early westward travel and settlement.
